EMPLOWER

Finding a job is hard. Women face additional challenges finding workplaces with an inclusive culture. Emplower helps women find tech companies to work for in Austin, TX that have been rated highly by other women. Emplower works by gathering anonymous company reviews (what people like and don't like about their company) and how a company scores on their workplace environment, benefits, and career advancement opportunities as ranked by other women. Everyone's values and preferences are different, that is why Emplower allows you to rank the importance of your search and recomends companys that maximizes the likelihood of company fit.
